About

     This web application addresses a lack of accessible information about voting experiences. This is important because not all polling locations conduct surveys or have formal methods of communication outside of social media which is largely unorganized. Although mainstream social media is on mode of expressing experiences, it is largely unorganized. Thus, MyVote aims to provide a more organized and structured form of sharing ones experiences and learning about others. Furthermore, this web applications strives to provide useful and pertinent feedback to government polling entities that may make use of voter feedback. This application would allow for much wider access of information of multiple polling places and may aid in building larger scale solutions to voting concerns, overall improving voting experiences.
